# Risk Limits

## Value at Risk (VaR)

* **Daily VaR limit**: 0.5% of portfolio value ($1,075,000 at current portfolio size)
* **Monthly VaR limit**: 2.0% of portfolio value ($4,300,000)
* Calculation method: Historical simulation, 95% confidence interval
* Lookback period: 252 trading days

## Duration Risk

* **Portfolio duration**: Maximum 2.5 years (weighted average)
* **DV01 limit**: $500,000 (dollar value of 1 basis point move)
* **Key rate duration**: No single maturity bucket > 40% of total DV01

## Credit Risk

* **Minimum credit rating**: Investment grade (BBB- or higher) for all holdings
* **Weighted average credit quality**: Must maintain A- or higher
* **Single issuer limit**: 15% of portfolio (non-government)
* **Single sector limit**: 40% of portfolio

## Liquidity Risk

* **Minimum liquid assets**: Must cover 150% of next 30-day obligations
* **Overnight liquidity**: Must exceed next-day obligations by $50M
* **Maximum illiquid allocation**: 10% of portfolio in assets with settlement > T+3

## Interest Rate Sensitivity

* **Parallel shift stress test**: Portfolio must withstand +200bps parallel shift
  * Maximum loss under stress: 5% of portfolio value
* **Curve steepening stress**: +100bps long end, -50bps short end
* **Curve flattening stress**: -50bps long end, +100bps short end

## Counterparty Risk

* **Maximum exposure per counterparty**: 25% of portfolio
* **Minimum counterparty rating**: A- for unsecured deposits
* **Collateral requirements**: 102% for repo transactions
